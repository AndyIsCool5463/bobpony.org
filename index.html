About Bob Pony

Bob Pony originally first started out as a simple YouTube channel that originally started back in early 2014. As of now, The Bob Pony YouTube channel has over 16,000 subscribers and over 9,000,000 views (as of 2018). The YouTube channel is mainly targeted to people who like technology, therefore videos of computer based content that are involved with operating systems and doing many random techy stuffs. As of now, the "Bob Pony" name has expanded ever since, which there's now this website, and many other social media services we're on.
